== Description == 

VW Dark is a fantastic WP theme that will be suitable for multiple business types and professions such as media agencies, fashion stores, designers, marketing experts, consultancies, creative designers, entrepreneurs, etc. Basically, you can use this theme as a multipurpose theme. It is minimal in style and crafted by expert developers for bringing a professional design that wonderfully works for your business. With an elegant page, you can also bring a sophisticated look that your audience will find equally enticing. Its clean layout will show your content in a most promising way and the responsive design will work magnificently for making your website perform well on every device including desktops, laptops, tablets as well as smartphones. It has a retina-ready display and a user-friendly design that both novices, as well as experts, can easily use for creating their websites. This beautiful free theme comes along with some personalization options as well. The core is packed with clean and secure HTML codes and has a mobile-friendly design. The theme is made SEO-friendly and gives faster page load time.  With the Bootstrap framework being used, it makes the theme robust and mobile-friendly. There are social media options available along with translation-ready design thanks to its WPML and RTL compatibility.
